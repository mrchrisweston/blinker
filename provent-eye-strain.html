How to prevent and reduce eye strain with the 20-20-20 rule.

Blinker was a weekend project created by Chris Weston

Many of us spend a good deal of our time staring at screens, from laptops, computers, smart phones, gaming systems and television which can put a lot of strain on our eyes or cause eye fatigue.

To reduce not only eye strain but neck, back and shoulder pain, it is important to take frequent breaks from your computer. This is where the 20-20-20 rule comes into play.

The 20-20-20 rule is simple; Every 20 minutes take a 20 second break and focus your eyes on something at least 20 feet away.

Simple.

Since it is nearly impossible for us, while engrossed in our work, to remember to have to take a break every 20 minutes, I created this handy little tool.

Introducing Blinker...

Use Blinker now →

Just open Blinker when you start your working day, hit start and leave it in the background for the rest of the day!

You will be notified every 20 minutes to take a break. And that's all that's to it! I hope you find it useful :)
